# Assessment Forms (project context)

The project has **3 source assessment forms**; ALPR implements **Form 3 only** this round. Context from `assessment_forms_reference.md`. All three share the same 4-level rubric and evidence-based approach (tally behaviors first, then judge the rubric level).

## The chain

```
นักวิจัย ──assess──► CAM (mentor) ──supervise/assess──► CAT (teacher) ──develop──► students (RL & CT)
   │                     │                                  │
 Form 2 (/20)        Form 1 (/52) classroom observation   learning outcomes
                     Form 3 (/20) AIPACK plan review  ◄── THIS SYSTEM (ALPR)
```

## Form 1 — Classroom Observation (พี่เลี้ยง/CAM) — /52 — *out of scope*
CAM observes CAT in class. Behavioral frequency tally (RL, CT, GM+PERMA) → rubrics: 4 overall dimensions (/16) + 9 deep indicators (/36) = /52.

## Form 2 — CAM Competency Evaluation (นักวิจัย) — /20 — *out of scope*
Researcher evaluates the CAM's coaching. Tally coaching behaviors → 5 rubric dimensions (/20) → mentor action triggers.

## Form 3 — AIPACK Lesson Plan Review (พี่เลี้ยง/CAM) — /20 — **implemented as ALPR**
CAM reviews the CAT's lesson plan:
- **Section 1** — basic info + evidence checklist (AI tool/KapiBarian, Prompt, Socratic questions, Analytic Rubric, thinking-trail worksheet).
- **Section 2** — 5 rubric components × 4 = /20 → these are **C1–C5** in [[AI Evaluation & Rubric]].
- **Section 3** — total /20 + PLC action triggers.
- **Section 4** — Strengths + Areas for Growth + CAM signature.

This maps directly onto the app: Section 1 → [[Upload-to-Report Pipeline|checklist]], Section 2 → the rubric, Section 3–4 → [[CAM Review & PDF Report|sign-off & report]].

## Out-of-scope this round (SRS §1.2)
- Forms 1 & 2, researcher/admin dashboards, cross-school statistics, fully-automated (no-human) evaluation.
